About the Event

Join us for the most anticipated academic event of the semester. The AI & Ethics Symposium brings together world-renowned researchers, industry leaders, and philosophers to tackle the pressing questions surrounding the rapid advancement of artificial intelligence.

From algorithmic bias to the future of cognitive labor, we will explore the intersection of technology and human values through a series of interactive panels and hands-on workshops.
